Hello. I like to call myself a digital artist. I do web and graphic development, and make vfx shorts and short videos and animations. In all my creations though, I try and design unique experiences.

I was born in 1996 in Delhi and have lived here ever since. Computers were my first love and I learnt a lot of different software and languages which I have elaborated on in the knowledge section. I am now pretty proficient in most of them and use my skills for various purposes. I have designed some useful apps like Typr, BrowserDzine and Greenhouse Emission Calculator. I wish to learn more computer science and make some contributions in the field of Brain Computer Interaction.

Although I love making things on the computer the most, I do a lot more in my free time. I love gaming on my XBOX 360 (which I won in a National Level Competition. Yay!) and watching films in any language. I also enjoy reading fiction, mystery thriller. Besides, I love running and jumping over bushes and stuff. I also hapen to be the President of Xino, the Co-Founder of Creativiz, the Co-Founder and Member of Brown n' Proud and the Creative Head at Nishtaa. I try and devote all time I get after all these activites to my studies and have so far managed to score well.

I am a fun person to talk to, and even more fun once you get to know the real me! Oh and yes! I get my best ideas in the toilet as at that time I can think peacefully.
